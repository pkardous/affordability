Housing Maps

The main map shows the proximity of public transportation (in pink) to different parcels owned by the City, County, State, and other tax-exempt organizations who may be interested in offering a portion of their property for new housing. This map is created with the input of citizens and organizations who are interested in partenering, and making use of their underutilized land.
